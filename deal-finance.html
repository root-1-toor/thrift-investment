Secondary Credit Market

Who We Finance

As one of the Mid-Atlantic region's largest financiers in the secondary automotive credit market, Thrift Investment is a seasoned specialist in hard-to-place secondary credit risk. We have the resources, know-how, and experience to extend credit to potential car purchasers who are refused by your primary loan sources.

Our assumption of secondary credit risk translates into higher profits for you, the retailer. Thrift wants to put its financing services and marketing strategies to work for you — turning previously missed opportunities into dramatically increased car sales.

Our team of skilled credit managers understands high-risk lending, the car business, and the retailers they serve. We will go the extra mile to make your deals happen.
